What does Phase Two involve?

On January 12th of 2023, at the beginning of this board’s tenure, we received a red violation notice from the City of Miami Beach for “Unsafe Structure”. This was based on the lack of a Recertification of Occupancy 40 year renewal that was due in 2017. To satisfy this violation, the association needed to successfully apply and be granted electrical and structural permits. This process, a long one, is soon to be finalized with the General Contractor based on the completed engineer’s report.

This has been the sole focus of the board throughout 2023, considered Phase One, as the most urgent and top priority to secure all of our investments.

Now that this is being finalized, we are excited to begin Phase Two. We plan to shift our focus to making improvements within our community. Important aspects like security, aesthetics, management, rules and regulations, as well as function and cleanliness are being given attention.

You will start to see small changes and we look forward to hearing your ideas on what you would like to see within the community.
