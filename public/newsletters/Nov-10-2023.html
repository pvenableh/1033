Happy Friday!

latest updates form the 1033 Lenox Board:

We are excited to report we have executed a contract to complete the work required by the 40 Year Recertification!

The scope includes required stucco restoration, exterior painting, required electrical repairs, parking lot re-paving and repairs to the back fence. The contract has been thoroughly reviewed by our attorney in order to protect our community. Our engineer and General Contractor will be working as a team to ensure that all Miami Beach requirements are met throughout the project.

Another exciting update...permits have been filed with the City of Miami Beach! Plus, our engineer has successfully registered our property with a new "Safe Structure" certificate of occupancy with the City.

What happens next?

Now that the 40 Year Recertification process is underway, we are excited to shift our focus to making improvements within our community. Important aspects like security, aesthetics, management and regulations, as well as function and cleanliness will be given attention.

You will start to see small changes and we look forward to hearing your ideas on what you would like to see within the community.

We will continually send updates on items such as financing, the Special Assessment, and more in upcoming communications. So...stay tuned.

We are looking forward to working together with everyone!

Reserves

We have been focused on building our reserves over the course of 2023. There is a new law requiring a significant amount of money to be in our reserves by the beginning of 2025. Our property manager is currently performing a reserve study.
